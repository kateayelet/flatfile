# FlatFile Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 28, 2026_

FlatFile is built to keep your data private. This policy explains what that means
in practice.

## What we collect

Nothing. FlatFile does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There is
no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs.

## Where your data lives

Your tables are stored as plain `.csv` files in storage you choose through the
Files app on iOS or Finder on Mac. FlatFile reads and writes those files in
place; it does not keep its own copy and it does not run a server. Your data is
never sent anywhere by FlatFile, because there is nowhere for it to go.

## iCloud and other storage

If you keep your files in iCloud Drive, Dropbox, or any other provider through
the Files app, that provider stores and syncs them under your own account, and
its handling of that data is governed by its own privacy policy. FlatFile only
sees the files you open, on your own device.

## Sharing and export

When you choose to export or share a table, the file goes only where you send it
using the standard share sheet (for example Files, Finder, Mail, or another app
you pick). FlatFile does not see or keep a copy of where you send it.

## Children

FlatFile does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new
date.
